Commitments

Our research rests on five intertwined commitments — from process-first thinking to responsible translation.

01

Process over system

What rules of interaction give rise to structure, cooperation, conflict, resilience, or collapse?

02

Eco-evolutionary-learning dynamics

Ecology, evolution and learning as coupled feedback systems on overlapping timescales.

03

Theory as a generative engine

Insight preceding application — conceptual clarity before intervention.

04

Cross-scale curiosity

From genes to societies — how higher-order structure arises from lower-level interactions.

05

Responsible translation

Connecting to resistance, agriculture, disease control, and cultural systems.
